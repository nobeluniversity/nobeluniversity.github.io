Nobel University

"Nobel University" is a name selected for a private research university for future establishment. The university is going be established in Chittagong, Bangladesh with a view to research and teaching. The university will focus on research, teaching methodology, and CQI (Continuous Quality Improvement) in education.

Mission & Vision

We, as faculty members of the university believe that education should be non-commercial and institute should really on research and quality education. Our mission is to establish an institute for educational research and publications. Bangladesh is a partially socialist democratic state. Most of the universities providing quality education to the nation are mostly state-owned. As a result, proper education and research are not being valued by the authorities rather than teaching in an old-fashioned way, where modern universities are focusing on more scientific research and development in teaching methodology. Our vision is to make an environment for students who are interested in their working field and build the future leaders of the world.
